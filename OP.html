About Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy", is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates



"海賊王に俺はなる!" (Kaizoku-o ni ore wa naru!) "I'm gonna be the Pirate King!"

Monkey D. Luffy's Characteristics

    He is
  1. Adventurous
  2. Fearlessness
  3. Carefree and Optimism
  4. Charisma
  5. Loyalty and Selflessness

Straw Hat Pirates

An infamous and incredibly powerful rising pirate crew that originated from the East Blue, but have various members from different areas. The members are:

  • Pirate Hunter Zoro
  • Cat Burglar Nami
  • God Usopp
  • Black Leg Sanji
  • Cotton Candy Lover Chopper
  • Devil Child Robin
  • Iron Man Franky
  • Soul King Brook
